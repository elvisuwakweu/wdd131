Site Name

Temple Explorer

This site name represents a project focused on temples worldwide, highlighting their history, location, and images.

Optional domain: templeexplorer.org

Site Purpose

The website provides a hub for learning about temples globally, including images, dedication dates, locations, and historical information for educational purposes.

Scenarios

  • Where can I see images and history of the Tokyo Japan Temple?
  • Which temples are located in the United States and when were they dedicated?

  • Orange (#E67E22): accents and highlights

Typography

  • Georgia: headings
